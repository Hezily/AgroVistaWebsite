How We Work

From soil to surplus — our four-step method

01

Soil Analysis

We begin every engagement with a full soil diagnostic — pH, nutrient profile, microbial activity, and moisture retention — mapped through our IoT sensor network and lab testing.

02

Custom Formulation

Based on the diagnostic, our scientists design a crop-specific input plan: the exact biostimulants, nutrients, and protection your land and crop variety need.

03

Field Deployment

Certified agronomists guide application on the ground, with vernacular-language support and precise scheduling tuned to your crop's growth stages.

04

Yield Monitoring

We track results through the full cycle — measuring yield gain, soil improvement, and ROI — then refine the plan for the next season. Continuous, data-driven growth.

Crop Nutrition
🌿

NutriMax Fertilizer Range

Precision-blended NPK formulations with slow-release technology that match crop-specific demand curves, reducing waste and boosting yield.

  • Crop-specific micro-nutrient ratios
  • 40% lower nitrogen run-off
  • Granule & liquid forms
  • Suitable for drip & broadcast
Crop Protection
🛡️

ShieldGuard Biopesticides

Plant extract-derived protection agents that target pest lifecycles without disrupting beneficial insects, pollinators, or food safety.

  • Non-toxic to bees & earthworms
  • 24-hour re-entry interval
  • Effective against 80+ pests
  • Residue-free by harvest

  • Integrates with mandi prices

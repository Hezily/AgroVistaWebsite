What We Offer

Products Built for Every Field

From soil enrichment to precision crop management, our portfolio addresses every stage of the agricultural cycle.

Soil Health
🌾

BioRich Soil Enhancers

Microbial inoculants and organic biostimulants that restore depleted soil microbiomes, improve water retention, and accelerate nutrient cycling — naturally.

  • Increases organic matter by up to 38%
  • Compatible with all crop types
  • Zero harmful residues
  • IARI certified formulations
Crop Nutrition
🌿

NutriMax Fertilizer Range

Precision-blended NPK formulations with slow-release technology that match crop-specific demand curves, reducing input waste and boosting yield consistency.

  • Crop-specific micro-nutrient ratios
  • 40% lower nitrogen run-off
  • Available in granule & liquid forms
  • Suitable for drip & broadcast
Crop Protection
🛡️

ShieldGuard Biopesticides

Plant extract-derived crop protection agents that target pest lifecycles without disrupting beneficial insects, pollinators, or downstream food safety.

  • Non-toxic to bees & earthworms
  • 24-hour re-entry interval only
  • Effective against 80+ pest species
  • Residue-free by harvest
Agri-Tech
📡

FieldPulse Smart Advisory

An IoT-integrated soil sensor network and AI-driven decision platform giving farmers real-time insights on irrigation, pest alerts, and market signals.

  • Real-time soil moisture & pH data
  • Vernacular-language mobile app
  • Works offline in low-signal areas
  • Integrates with mandi price data
Seeds
🌱

HybridCore Seed Varieties

Drought-tolerant, high-yielding hybrid seeds developed through marker-assisted breeding for crops adapted to Indian growing zones.

  • Germination rate above 92%
  • Resistant to blast, rust & blight
  • Shorter maturity cycles
  • Field-trialled across 5 agro-climates

AgroVista Expert Network

  • 500+ certified field agronomists
